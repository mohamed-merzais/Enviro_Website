Who are we:

We are a non-profit organisation made to protect this earth. To this date the earth is in critical condition due to the activity of human beings. From using simple everyday life items, the earth is being harmed. Greenhouse gasses are harming the ozone layer which absorbs most of the biologically damaging ultraviolet sunlight and only allowing a small amountr to reach the earth’s surface. This absorption of ultraviolet rays allows a source of heat.

So without the ozone layer more biologically harmful ultraviolet rays will reach the earth’s surface and will come in contact with animals. This increases the rate in sunburn and skin cancer. These are catastrophic consequences if the ozone layer was to become thinner.

Due to the increase of greenhouse gasses, they are responsible for the depleting of the ozone layer as they attack and destroy it.

This is where we as an organisation come in and try to reduce the amount of greenhouse gasses emitted into the atmosphere.

There are also other problems that the earth is facing such as:

  • Air pollution
  • Climate change
  • Litter
  • Waste
  • Soil contamination

These are examples of a few problems that we humans are facing today which In effect is having a huge impact on the planet we are on.
Environmental issues are harmful effects of human activity on the biophysical environment. Environmental protection is a practice of protecting the natural environment for the benefit of both the environment and humans.

What do we do:

We undergo various projects to try and reduce the emission of greenhouse gasses and other projects to help the environment:

  • we clean oceans to help animals from being harmed and to have cleaner oceans
  • we make sure that we plant 2 trees for everyone that is cut down
  • we make sure that the water that comes to everyone's house is filtered properly
  • we also provide services to new businesses and products:
